Objectives

Enhance Practical Learning:To provide students with an interactive platform where they can perform virtual experiments, bridging the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical application.

Improve Accessibility and Flexibility:To allow students to access lab experiments anytime, anywhere, without being limited by physical infrastructure or lab timings.

Promote Conceptual Understanding:To help students understand complex engineering and technical concepts through real-time simulations, visualizations, and hands-on virtual practice.
